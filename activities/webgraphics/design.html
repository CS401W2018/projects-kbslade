What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

Photos that have a lot of color and detail - like high resolution photos.

 

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

Pictures that have crisp lines and few colors (blocks of the same color), like logos, animation, etc. as well as smaller pictures.

 

What is unique about the PNG format?

You don't lose quality when the photo is compressed. It's good for storing high quality photos and images.

 

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

You use a photo editor you find online.

Then, when putting it on your page, you can adjust the height or width (or both) by adding an id to the photo, then adding height in the css page.

 

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

When you're resizing photos, especially large high quality photos to small, the photo editing software better converts the photos. CSS is more rudimentary compared to using more specialized software.
