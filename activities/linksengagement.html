Absolute vs Relative Links

Absolute links are links to websites

Relative links are links to different parts within your website.

Relative links have 3 parts:

  1. One (.) period means current folder (not used much)
  2. Two (..) periods means parent folder
  3. slash (/) (at the beginning) means start at the root

Links can but do not need to be in the navigation bar.
