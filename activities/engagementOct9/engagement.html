What is the margin, border, and padding?

The margin is the transparent area outside of the border.

The border surrounds the content and is outside the padding.

The padding is transparent. It's space between the border and the content.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

You use pixels or px as measurement in CSS. You can also use mm, cm, in, or even percentages.

When would you use a figure tag?

When you have a figure (like a table, image, etc) to make it become a block tag- so itll take the full width of the screen and make the text fall either above or below the tag.
