What does each website have?

  • A Header! Headers are on every page on the website.
  • Navigation bar (nav)
  • Links (usually on the top part of the website). They DON'T change order. We use navigation (or nav). You never link stuff from other websites- only link to your own webpage. Links do not need to be in the nav bar.
  • main body (main)
  • footer (footer)

Reminder: we use HTML for Google to search and bring it closer to the top of the search results.
