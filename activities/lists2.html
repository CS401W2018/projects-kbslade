How to get a good grade in class:

  • Start Early on your projects.
  • Plan ahead that projects take time and work hard on the project.
  • Finish the project by Monday, at the beginning of class, so that you can ask questions you may have in class.

When are projects due?

Projects are due on Mondays.

Special entity characters:

  1. empasis: em
  2. bold: strong
  3. break lines: br
  4. non-breaking space:   to get more spaces than 1 in a line
  5. UL un-ordered list (order doesn't matter): ul then use li
  6. OL ranking list with numbers: ol then use li
  7. ranking list with letters: add attribute I.e. "ol type="a" or use "I" for Roman numberals at the beginning of the list
  8. special entity characters: Have an "&" at the beginning and end with a ";". You can get a list of them online.
