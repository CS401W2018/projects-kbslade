Internet

Definition: the internet is a group or network of computers and electronic devices that share information and communicate with one another.

Web

Definition: a system that allows us to share information and content over the internet. It's the documents and links that are shared over the internet, or network. Another name is the World Wide Web (or www).

How are the Internet and Web different?

The Internet is the network that connects the computers and devices whereas the web is the information, links, and databases.

ASCII and Unicode

Definition: Unicode is universal characters used on a computer that translates binary to language we humans can understand. ASCII is 7 or 8-bit binary numbers that represent English, so we can communicate with the computer.

What is UTF-8?

UTF-8 is an encoding system used in Unicode. It translates numbers to binary
