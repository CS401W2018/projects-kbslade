Ideas from Podcasts

One of my favorite podcasts is called Follow Him. There are two hosts, Hank Smith and John Bytheway, who discuss sections of scripture with different scholars of many different areas of study, from archaeologists, linguists, human and family scientists, psychologists, professors of religious studies, etc. Though I've enjoyed just about everything and learned a lot, the following are some of my top favorite ideas to share with others.

Standards

  • "Fence Laws" from John Hilton III Follow Him, NT, episode 12
  • Righteous Judgement from Lili Anderson Follow Him, NT, episode 9

Media Choices

  • Anxiety and Media from Matt Townsend, Follow Him, BOM, episode 41
  • Affects of Violence from Larry Nelson, Follow Him, BOM, episode 44

Family Council

  • Teaching and Rearing Children from Jenet Erickson, Follow Him, OT, epidsode 4
  • Relationships (Plan metaphors - family model with Christ) from Matt Townsend, Follow Him, NT, episode 20
  • Priesthood Power from Barbara Gardner, Follow Him, D&C, ep 31
