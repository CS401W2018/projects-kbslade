Using Media in Learning and Teaching

There are many different forms of media that can be used to enhance teaching. Since many people learn through different ways, media can be a valuable teaching tool.

Pictures

Pictures can help students to visualize a concept, give a visual as they listen to a lesson, or provide understanding to a concept. One example is from my religion class. Our teacher showed us a picture he took of Babylonian ruins in the foreground with Sadam Hussein's palace in the background which showed a possible interpretation of the scripture we were studying.

Music

Music can also be powerful contributor to learning and teaching concepts. For example, in one of my religion classes about Isaiah, our teacher had us listen to this song. It was a beautiful way that brought the text to life for me and increased in my understanding of it.
